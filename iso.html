FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ISO certification for your business.

1
What is ISO certification?
ISO (International Organization for Standardization) certification demonstrates that your business meets internationally recognized standards for quality, safety, and efficiency in your industry.
2
Which ISO should I get?
ISO 9001 (Quality Management) is most universal. ISO 27001 is for IT/data companies. ISO 14001 is for environment-conscious businesses. ISO 45001 is for workplace safety. We guide you to the right one.
3
How long is ISO certification valid?
ISO certification is valid for 3 years with mandatory annual surveillance audits. After 3 years, a recertification audit is required.
4
What are the benefits of ISO certification?
Increased customer trust, competitive advantage in tenders, access to global markets, improved operational efficiency, and eligibility for government contracts.
