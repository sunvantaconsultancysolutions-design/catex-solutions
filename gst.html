Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GST registration

1
Who needs GST registration?
Businesses with annual turnover exceeding ₹40 lakhs (₹20 lakhs for services, ₹10 lakhs for special category states) must register for GST. Interstate sellers and e-commerce sellers must register regardless of turnover.
2
How long does GST registration take?
GST registration typically takes 3–7 working days after successful document submission. You receive an ARN (Application Reference Number) immediately upon filing.
3
What documents are required?
PAN card, Aadhaar card, bank statement/cancelled cheque, business registration proof, address proof of business premises, and passport-size photographs are required.
4
What is the penalty for not registering?
Penalty is 10% of the tax due (minimum ₹10,000). For deliberate fraud, penalty can be 100% of the tax amount.
5
Can I voluntarily register for GST?
Yes, businesses below the threshold can voluntarily register for GST to claim input tax credit and appear more credible to larger clients.
