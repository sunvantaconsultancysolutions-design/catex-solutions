Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Udyam / MSME registration

1
Who is eligible for Udyam Registration?
Micro: Investment ≤₹1 Cr & Turnover ≤₹5 Cr. Small: Investment ≤₹10 Cr & Turnover ≤₹50 Cr. Medium: Investment ≤₹50 Cr & Turnover ≤₹250 Cr.
2
What are the benefits of Udyam Registration?
Priority lending from banks, government subsidies, protection against delayed payments under MSMED Act, preference in government tenders, concession on electricity bills, and more.
3
Is Udyam Registration free?
Registration on the Udyam portal is free of government fee. However, professional assistance for error-free filing is recommended.
4
How long is Udyam Registration valid?
Udyam Registration is valid for a lifetime unless cancelled. However, the classification may change if turnover or investment crosses the threshold.
5
What documents are needed?
Aadhaar card of the proprietor/director/partner, PAN card, GST number (if applicable), and bank account details are required.
