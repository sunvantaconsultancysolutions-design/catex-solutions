Refund & Cancellation

Refund Policy

We believe in transparency. Here's exactly what you can expect if you need to cancel or request a refund for our services.

Last Updated: May 2024

Our Commitment to Fairness

At Captex Company, we understand that plans change. Our refund policy is designed to be fair to both our clients and our team. Refunds are calculated based on the stage at which your service is when the cancellation is requested. All refund decisions are final and processed within 7–10 business days.

Refund Schedule

Cancellation Stage Refund Amount Details
Within 24 Hours of Payment 90% Refund Full refund minus a small administrative processing fee of 10%. No questions asked.
After 24 Hours — Before Work Starts 75% Refund If cancelled after 24 hours but before our team has initiated any work on your case, 75% of the service fee will be refunded.
After Work Has Started 50% Refund Once our team has commenced document preparation, review, or filing assistance, a 50% refund of the service fee will be issued.
After Government Fees Paid No Refund on Govt. Fees Government filing fees, stamp duty, or statutory charges paid to authorities on your behalf are strictly non-refundable. Service fee refund may still apply based on stage.
After Filing is Completed No Refund Once the application or filing has been submitted to the relevant government portal or authority, no refund can be processed.

Processing Timeline

24–48 hrs

Refund request acknowledgement after email received

3–5 days

Review and verification of cancellation request

7–10 days

Refund credited to original payment method

Non-Refundable Items

  • Government filing fees, stamp duty, or statutory charges paid to any authority on your behalf.
  • Third-party costs such as notarization, attestation, courier, or translation charges.
  • Fees for completed services where filing or submission has been made.
  • Emergency or priority processing fees once the work has commenced.

How to Request a Refund

1

Send a Refund Request Email

Email us at refund@captexcompany.com with your full name, order ID or service reference number, date of payment, and reason for cancellation.

2

Receive Acknowledgement

Our team will acknowledge your refund request within 24–48 business hours and provide a reference number for tracking purposes.

3

Review & Verification

We will assess the stage of your service and verify applicable refund amounts as per our refund schedule. We may contact you for clarification if needed.

4

Refund Approval & Processing

Upon approval, the refund will be initiated to your original payment method within 7–10 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once processed.
