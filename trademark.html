Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about trademark registration

1
What can be registered as a trademark?
Words, names, logos, slogans, sounds, colours, shapes, or any combination that distinguishes your goods/services from others can be registered as a trademark.
2
How long is a trademark valid?
A trademark registration is valid for 10 years from the date of filing and can be renewed indefinitely for further 10-year periods by paying the renewal fee.
3
How long does trademark registration take?
The complete process takes 18–24 months. However, you get TM status (right to use ™ symbol) immediately after filing. The ® symbol is used only after final registration.
4
What is the difference between TM and R symbol?
™ (TM) symbol can be used once you've filed your trademark application. ® (Registered) symbol can only be used after your trademark is officially registered by the Trademark Registry.
5
Can I file in multiple classes?
Yes, you should file in all relevant classes where your goods/services fall. Each class requires a separate application and fee.
