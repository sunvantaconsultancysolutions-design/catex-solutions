Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about closing a Private Limited Company.

Fast Track Exit (STK-2 form) for dormant/inactive companies, or winding up through NCLT for companies with operations/liabilities.
Company must have nil assets/liabilities, must not have been active for 1-2 years, all pending returns must be filed, and majority directors must consent.
Fast track closure typically takes 90-120 days. NCLT winding up can take 1-3 years.
All pending annual returns (MGT-7, AOC-4) must be filed before applying for closure. We assist with filing all pending compliances.
