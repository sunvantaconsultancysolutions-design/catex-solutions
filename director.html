The person must have a valid DIN, must not be disqualified under the Companies Act, must give consent in Form DIR-2, and must not be an undischarged insolvent.
A Private Limited Company can have minimum 2 and maximum 15 directors. Additional directors beyond 15 require shareholder approval via special resolution.
Yes, every director must have a Director Identification Number (DIN). We can assist with DIN application if the incoming director doesn't have one.
DIR-12 is the MCA form that notifies the Registrar of Companies about the appointment of a new director. It must be filed within 30 days of appointment.
