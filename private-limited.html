FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a Private Limited Company?

A Private Limited Company is a type of company that limits owner liability to their shares, limits the number of shareholders to 200, and restricts shareholders from publicly trading shares. It is the most popular form of business entity in India.

2. What is the minimum capital required?

There is no minimum paid-up capital requirement for a Private Limited Company in India after the Companies Act 2013 amendment. You can start with any amount.

3. How many directors are required?

A minimum of 2 directors are required for a Private Limited Company, with a maximum of 15 directors. At least one director must be a resident of India.

4. How long does registration take?

Typically 10-15 working days from the date of document submission, subject to MCA processing times and document correctness.

5. What documents are required?

PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, latest bank statement (last 2 months), address proof, passport-size photograph of all directors, and proof of registered office address are required.
